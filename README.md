Battle Fortress Game – README
Overview

Battle Fortress Game is a multiplayer 2D strategy and combat game where players build, defend, and attack fortresses in an enemy-versus-ally battlefield. Players must cooperate with teammates, manage resources, strengthen defenses, and destroy enemy bases to achieve victory.

The game combines real-time combat, base defense, resource management, and team strategy into a competitive experience.

Features ~

Fortress Building – Construct walls, towers, and defensive structures.
Attack & Defense Gameplay – Defend your base while launching attacks on enemy fortresses.
Character Combat System – Players can move, attack, and use weapons or special abilities.
Health System – Units and structures have health points and can be destroyed.
Resource Collection – Gather resources to upgrade fortifications and unlock stronger equipment.
Enemy AI (Optional) – Computer-controlled enemies for single-player or training mode.
Leaderboard & Score System – Track player performance and match results.
Game Services – Login system, matchmaking, save/load progress, and online rooms.
Gameplay
Players join a match.
Teams spawn inside their fortress.
Collect resources and upgrade defenses.
Attack enemy players and destroy their structures.
Protect your own fortress from enemy invasions.
The team that destroys the enemy main fortress wins.
Technologies Used
